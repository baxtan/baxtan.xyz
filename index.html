Amir Bastani

As a new media artist with a background in fine arts and philosophy, Amir Bastani (*1991) explores the gap between the conscious and the subconscious. He realises his works by designing narratives through real-time processes.
Amir is based in Linz/AT, where he is pursuing his PhD and is currently a researcher at Creative Robotics and an external lecturer at the Kunstuni Linz.
